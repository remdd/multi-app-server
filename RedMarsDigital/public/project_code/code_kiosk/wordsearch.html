How to play

The names of twelve of the Wildlife Oasis' inhabitants are hidden in each wordsearch.

Silhouettes of the hidden animals are shown on either side of the grid. See how many you can recognize just from these clues!

Be warned though - some of the inhabitants are very hard to name just from their silhouettes. If you get stuck, tap the hint button at the bottom of the screen to see the names that are still hidden.

Names can read from top to bottom, from left to right or diagonally from left to right and either up or down.

When you find a hidden name, you can select it by:

  • tapping the first letter, then the last letter of the name
  • ...or by dragging your finger from first to last letter.

If you are right, a ring will be drawn around the word and the animal's silhouette will be replaced by a colour picture.
