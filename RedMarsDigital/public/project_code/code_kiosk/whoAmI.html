    How to play

    Using the clues, try to work out which animal is being described, from the choices at the bottom of the screen.

    A new clue will be revealed every 12 seconds - and will sometimes be accompanied by a picture.

    Your score each round is based on how long you take to pick the right animal. Be careful though, you will lose points for each wrong guess!

    After 5 rounds you will be shown your overall score. See if you can beat it next time!
