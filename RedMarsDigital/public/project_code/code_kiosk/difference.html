How to play


  • First, pick a picture from the options on the right.

  • You will then be shown two copies of the same picture side by side, but they will not quite be the same... the one on the right has been edited!

  • When you spot a difference between the two pictures, tap its location in the right-hand picture.

  • The paw prints below the pictures will show you how many differences you have left to find.

  • Try to find all the differences as quickly as you can!
